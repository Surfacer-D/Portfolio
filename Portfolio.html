About Spamandla Mdunyelwa

Spamandla Mdunyelwa is a passionate self-taught surface pattern designer behind DZIRIBA. Her journey in the world of design began to gain recognition in 2018 when she became one of the Top 10 finalists in the Nando's Hot Young Pattern Designer Competition. This achievement earned her a coveted spot as a Design Maker on the Nando's Portal to Africa, making her patterns available to international designers for use in Nando's restaurants worldwide.

For six years, Spamandla has been on a journey of learning and unlearning in the pattern and textile industry, a path that has brought her ineffable joy and inspiration. Her work under the brand name DZIRIBA showcases her growth and passion for cultural expression through design.

The Inspiration Behind ISAZISI (Identity)

"My designs embody a deep yearning for cultural connection, stemming from my desire to embrace my heritage. Seasonal gatherings during December holidays in rural areas left me craving a stronger bond. Inspired by the nostalgic melodies and captivating dances of my culture, isiXhosa, I embark on a journey to continuously design patterns that weave together fragments of my identity."

Spamandla's artwork is characterized by repeating lines and circles reminiscent of Xhosa attire and ancestral facial art (dot work), reflecting unity. Her designs bridge urban life with the traditions of her tribe, creating a bond where she finds belonging while honoring the legacy of her people.
